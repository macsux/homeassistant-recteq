## RecTeq for Home Assistant

Local-only integration for RecTeq Wi-Fi grills. Sign in once with your RecTeq app credentials and your grill's switches, setpoints, pit temp, and four probe temps appear as Home Assistant entities — talking to the grill directly over your LAN, no cloud, no Frida, no key copying.

Currently supports the **DualFire 2000**. Other RecTeq models welcome — open an issue with your `productId`.
